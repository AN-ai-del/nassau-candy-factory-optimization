# 📊 Executive Summary

---

# Project Overview

The **Factory Optimization Dashboard** is an AI-powered business decision support system developed to predict factory profitability and recommend operational strategies using Machine Learning.

The project combines predictive analytics, business optimization, and interactive visualization into a unified dashboard that assists decision-makers in maximizing profitability.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objective is to improve operational decision-making by predicting the expected profit of customer orders before production and shipment decisions are made.

The system enables managers to:

- Predict order profitability
- Identify high-value opportunities
- Optimize shipping decisions
- Improve regional allocation
- Support production planning

---

# Solution

The project integrates:

- Machine Learning
- Business Intelligence
- Interactive Dashboard
- Recommendation Engine
- Strategy Optimizer

Users can evaluate operational scenarios, compare strategies, and receive AI-generated business recommendations in real time.
